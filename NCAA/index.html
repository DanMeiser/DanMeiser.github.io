📊 BLENDED ANALYTICAL MODEL — 4 SIGNALS, ADJUSTABLE WEIGHTS

Picks are driven by a blended model combining four independent signals, each contributing a weighted share to the final win probability. KenPom (default 50%) uses adjusted efficiency margins — points scored and allowed per 100 possessions, pace-adjusted. Feature Combination (25%) layers in tempo mismatches, turnover rate, four-factor matchup analysis (eFG%, ORB%, FTR), and coach tournament experience. MM Regression (25%) blends historic seed-based upset rates with efficiency to capture March-specific pressure patterns. Seed History (default 0%) uses pure 40-year historical win rates by seed matchup — no team quality adjustment. Round History (default 20%) applies per-round survival rates from 40 years of data: #1 seeds win 84% of R32 games, 72% of S16 games, 65% of E8 games, and only 47% of Final Four games — naturally compressing favorites in later rounds without randomness. Use the sliders below to shift the model toward any signal and see picks update in real time.
